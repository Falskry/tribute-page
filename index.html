Michael Faraday, 1842

Here are some key dates from Faraday's life

  • 1791: Born in Newington Butts
  • 1805: Become the apprentice of a book binder and book seller
  • 1805-1812: Read lot of books about science
  • 1813: Become the assistant of Humphrey Davy, an english chemist
  • 1821:Marries Sarah Barnard
  • 1821:discovers magnetism
  • 1825:becomes director of the laboratory of the royal institution
  • 1831:discovers electromagnetic induction
  • 1832:investigate electricity laws and find out that there is only one type of electricity
  • 1843:becomes the first Fullerian professor of chemistry
  • 1845:discovers diamagnetism
  • 1851:participate in the planning and and is a judge for the London Great Exhibition
  • 1867:dies, aged 75.
